Bus Body Rubber Beading

To give perfect fitting to the glass, mud guard and many other parts of buses, here we are offering Bus Body Fitting Rubber. Anti-abrasive in nature, this one is also installed to guard buses from damages like scratches, dents & breakage that may harm it during accidents. The absolutely simple to install rubber products comes in several thicknesses to fit into several buses models & designs. Made employing latest extrusion methods, the array promises excellent level of air and water tightness. Most of the front or back glasses of the bus are installed using this kind of Bus Body Fitting Rubber.

Features

Durability

Wear & tear resistive and flexible but strong, ensuring long-lasting protection for your vehicle

Weather Resistant

Capable of withstanding various conditions like vibration, climate & aerodynamic effects

Chemical Resistant

Possesses high chemicals and impact resistance for maximum protection

Easy Maintenance

Can be washed to have new like finishing for years, ensuring lasting appearance
