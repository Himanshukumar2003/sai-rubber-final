PVC Water Stopper Rubber Seal

One of the most used sealing products across the marine, engineering, automotive and industrial equipment is Rubber Seals/ Compression Seals. Whether you are looking for a material which promises to protect your product or premise from vibration, noise, diverse weather conditions like heat, chilling winters breeze & rains and several atmospheric dust elements, this one is perfect for you to buy. Strip Seal

Strip Seal is used to lessen the impact of the bundle bypass stream, which circles the exterior of the tube bundle. In order to stop the bypass flow and force it back into the tube bundle, they are typically thin strips of metal that slide into slots in the baffles and extend outward toward the shell wall.
