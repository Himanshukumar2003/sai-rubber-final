Product Description

Owing to our state-of-art manufacturing unit and cutting edge technology, we are engaged in manufacturing and exporting a wide range of Glass Rubber Beading. Manufactured using high grade raw rubber and advanced techniques as per the set industry norms, these beadings are highly admired by the clients all across the globe. This Glass Rubber Beading is widely used in extruder products and in the front & side glasses of automobiles.Our Glass Rubber Beading is perfect for craft projects and home decorations. Our beading features high-quality, glossy glass beads combined with flexible rubber strands to create a unique texture and color palette. With hundreds of designs, you are sure to find something that meets your aesthetic goals. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced craftsperson, our beading will give any project a professional and creative touch.
